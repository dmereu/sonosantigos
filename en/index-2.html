"Sonos Antigos de Sardegna" it's a musical project, focused on spread the traditional Sardinian music and culture.
The project was born from the joining of two virtuous musicians, sharing their passion for popular culture of their island. The melodies of "launeddas" played by Massimo Congiu are completed with the pace dectated rhythm of the accordeon played by Davide Mereu, recreating that old musical alchemy that since lots of generations keep alive the deep bond between Sardinian people and their land.
